# 🌱 Energy & environment APIs

Energy, carbon, air quality, and sustainability data.

| API | Description | Auth | HTTPS | Docs |
|-----|-------------|------|-------|------|
| Electricity Maps | Real-time grid carbon intensity | apiKey | Yes | https://portal.electricitymaps.com/docs/ |
| WattTime | Marginal grid emissions signals | apiKey | Yes | https://docs.watttime.org/ |
| UK Carbon Intensity | GB electricity carbon intensity | No | Yes | https://carbonintensity.org.uk/ |
| Carbon Interface | Carbon-footprint estimates | apiKey | Yes | https://docs.carboninterface.com/ |
| EIA (US Energy Info) | US energy production & prices | apiKey | Yes | https://www.eia.gov/opendata/ |
| NREL | US renewable-energy & solar data | apiKey | Yes | https://developer.nrel.gov/docs/ |
| OpenAQ | Open global air-quality data | apiKey | Yes | https://docs.openaq.org/ |
| IQAir (AirVisual) | Air quality & pollution | apiKey | Yes | https://www.iqair.com/air-pollution-data-api |
| OpenWeather Air Pollution | Air-quality index & components | apiKey | Yes | https://openweathermap.org/api/air-pollution |
| PVGIS (EU) | Solar photovoltaic performance | No | Yes | https://joint-research-centre.ec.europa.eu/pvgis-online-tool_en |
| Global Forest Watch | Forest change & land data | apiKey | Yes | https://www.globalforestwatch.org/help/developers/ |
| USGS Water Services | US water & streamflow data | No | Yes | https://waterservices.usgs.gov/ |
| Ambee | Air, weather, pollen, fire data | apiKey | Yes | https://www.getambee.com/api |
